About Yang Yum

而出生在能看見龜山島各個面貌的地方，原本讀著電機，讀著讀著就去做餐飲了，
我是一位對美食有著熱誠的人，喜歡品嘗各種不同的料理和甜點，是促使我一步一步前進的動力
除了工作之外，工作之餘喜歡分享自己對美食和料理的見解，並透過不同的角度觀察人群和解構美食。

揚揚食晤是我所建立的工作室
想透過不一樣的角度，觀察人群，解構美食。
每個人對美食的定義不一樣，但生活中處處都會有驚喜。

岳
台灣是一個70%都是山的大島，許多人也熱愛著我們的山林，
就像我們的塔賦予的一座座小小山峰中，花草茶叢生般的多樣。
我想要透過這樣的氛圍來呈現我最棒的作品！


認識我後，除了烹飪和美食，你也有其他的興趣嗎？或是有什麼夢想或目標嗎？也跟我分享和探索你的美食故事吧！

I was born in a place where I could see various faces of Guishan Island. Originally studying electrical engineering, I eventually ventured into the culinary industry due to my passion for food and cooking. Apart from my work, I enjoy sharing my thoughts on food and observing people's relationship with it from different perspectives.

I established a studio called "Yang Yum" with the aim of deconstructing food and observing people's relationship with it from different angles. Everyone has a different definition of what good food is, but there are always surprises in life. Regarding the mountains in Taiwan, which account for 70% of the island, many people appreciate our natural surroundings. Just like the diversity of flowers and tea plants in the small peaks that are endowed by the Tree, I want to present my best work in such an environment.

After getting to know me, do you have any other interests apart from cooking and food? What are your dreams or goals? Let's share and explore our food stories together!
